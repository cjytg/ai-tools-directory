# Midjourney Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Midjourney for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Midjourney is ai image generation tool known for artistic quality. At $10-60/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Midjourney?

Midjourney is made by Midjourney, founded in 2022. It's designed for designers, artists, marketers, content creators, and it shows. The tool focuses on art creation, design concepts, marketing visuals, illustrations.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-image**
- **Style reference**
- **Upscaling**
- **Variations**

The features are solid. I particularly like the text-to-image—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

- **Rating:** ★ 4.7/5

If you're a designers looking for art creation, Midjourney is worth a look. It's particularly good for design concepts.
