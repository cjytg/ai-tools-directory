# Speechify Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Speechify for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Speechify is text-to-speech app for reading articles, documents, and books. At $139/year, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Speechify?

Speechify is made by Speechify, founded in 2017. It's designed for students, professionals, people with dyslexia, and it shows. The tool focuses on reading assistance, accessibility, learning, productivity.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-speech**
- **Speed control**
- **Mobile app**
- **Chrome extension**

The features are solid. I particularly like the text-to-speech—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $139/year
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.0/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Mobile app

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

## Who Should Use Speechify?

If you're a students looking for reading assistance, Speechify is worth a look. It's particularly good for accessibility.

**Skip it if:** You're on a tight budget or need something more powerful. Check out ElevenLabs, Murf instead.
