# Coda Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Coda for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** Coda is an All-in-one doc that combines docs, spreadsheets, and apps. At $10/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Coda?

Coda is made by Coda, founded in 2014. It's designed for teams, product managers, and it works well for docs, databases.

## Key Features

- **Docs**
- **Tables**
- **Automation**
- **Packs**

The features are solid. I particularly like the docs—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $10/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.4/5

## Pros

- Very flexible
- Good automation
- All-in-one

## Cons

- Learning curve
- Can be slow
- Complex pricing

## Who Should Use Coda?

If you're a teams looking for docs, Coda is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out Notion AI, Airtable, Google Docs instead.

## Alternatives

Notion AI, Airtable, Google Docs are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

Coda delivers on its promises. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.4/5**
