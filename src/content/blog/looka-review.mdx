# Looka Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Looka for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Looka is an AI-powered logo and brand identity generator. At $20 one-time, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Looka?

Looka is made by Looka, founded in 2016. It's designed for startups, small businesses, entrepreneurs, and it shows. The tool focuses on logo design, brand identity, startups, quick branding.

## Key Features

- **Logo generation**
- **Brand kit**
- **Social media**
- **Business cards**

The features are solid. I particularly like the logo generation—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $20 one-time
- **Free tier:** No
- **Rating:** ★ 4.0/5

You'll need to pay to get started.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Fast logo creation
- Affordable
- Brand kit included

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Less unique than custom
- Limited customization
- Stock-like feel

 But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Looka?

If you're a startups looking for logo design, Looka is worth a look. It's particularly good for brand identity.
