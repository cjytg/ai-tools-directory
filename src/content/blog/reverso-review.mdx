## Intro

Reverso is a comprehensive AI-powered language platform that goes beyond simple word-for-word translation to provide deep linguistic context, usage examples, and language learning tools. Covering 15+ languages, Reverso's translation engine shows how words and phrases are actually used in real-world contexts, drawing from a massive database of bilingual texts and professional documents. The platform's Context feature is particularly powerful — when you translate a word, Reverso displays dozens of real examples showing that exact translation in sentences from verified bilingual sources, helping users understand nuance and appropriate usage. Reverso's Conjugation tool provides complete verb tables across all tenses, while its Synonym Finder helps users discover alternative expressions to enrich their writing. The Grammar checker identifies and explains language errors, making Reverso valuable for active language learning and professional writing. Reverso offers a robust free tier, while premium plans unlock unlimited context searches and advanced grammar correction. The platform is widely used by language students, translators, and international professionals who need more than basic translation — they need to understand how language actually works in practice.

## What Is Reverso?

Reverso has built a comprehensive language platform that combines translation, conjugation, grammar checking, and language learning tools into a single ecosystem, making it useful for both translation tasks and language study. The platform's context-based translation feature shows how phrases are used in real-world sentences drawn from professional translations, which helps users understand not just what a word means but how it's actually used by native speakers. Its conjugation tables, grammar exercises, and vocabulary building tools make it a genuinely useful language learning resource, not just a translation utility.

## Features Deep Dive

### Contextual translation

Reverso's signature feature shows how words and phrases are actually used in real-world sentences drawn from professional translations and bilingual texts. When you translate a word, you see dozens of contextual examples that reveal nuance and appropriate usage.

### Conjugation

Comprehensive verb conjugation tables across all tenses and moods help language learners master verb forms. The tool covers 15+ languages with complete paradigm charts.

### Examples

Real-world usage examples are drawn from verified bilingual sources including literature, news, and professional documents. This helps users understand not just what a word means, but how native speakers actually use it.

### Grammar checker

Built-in grammar checking identifies and explains language errors across multiple languages. It's particularly useful for active language learning and professional writing in a non-native language.
