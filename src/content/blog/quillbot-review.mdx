# QuillBot Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using QuillBot for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** QuillBot is an AI paraphrasing and writing tool for improving text. At $9.95/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is QuillBot?

QuillBot is made by QuillBot, founded in 2017. It's designed for students, researchers, non-native speakers, and it shows. The tool focuses on academic writing, paraphrasing, summarizing, citations.

## Key Features

- **Paraphraser**
- **Grammar checker**
- **Summarizer**
- **Citation generator**

The features are solid. I particularly like the paraphraser—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $9.95/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.1/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Great for paraphrasing
- Free tier useful
- Simple interface

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Limited free modes
- Can change meaning
- Not for long-form

 But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use QuillBot?

If you're a students looking for academic writing, QuillBot is worth a look. It's particularly good for paraphrasing.

**Skip it if:** Check out Grammarly, ChatGPT, DeepL instead.

## The Bottom Line

QuillBot is decent but not amazing. It's not perfect—the limited free modes is annoying—but overall, there are better options out there.
