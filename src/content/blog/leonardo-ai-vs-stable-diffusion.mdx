## What Is Leonardo AI?

**Leonardo AI** is built by Leonardo AI. AI image generation platform with fine-tuning capabilities.

Leonardo AI is a comprehensive AI art generation platform designed for creators, game developers, and digital artists who need fine-grained control over their outputs. The platform offers a suite of fine-tuned models optimized for specific use cases, including game asset creation, character design, environmental art, and concept illustration. Leonardo AI provides advanced features such as image-to-image generation, canvas editing, model training on custom datasets, and real-time generation for rapid iteration. The platform's free tier grants users 150 tokens daily, allowing consistent access without payment, while the premium plan at approximately $10 per month unlocks significantly higher token allocations, priority processing, and access to all available models. Leonardo AI's model marketplace lets users discover and apply community-trained models for specialized styles and subjects. The platform strikes a balance between powerful creative tools and an approachable interface, making it suitable for both professional artists building game assets and hobbyists exploring AI-generated art.

## What Is Stable Diffusion?

**Stable Diffusion** is developed by Stability AI. Open-source AI image generation model that runs locally.

Stable Diffusion Online provides web-based access to the open-source Stable Diffusion model without requiring any local installation, GPU hardware, or technical configuration. This accessibility makes it an ideal starting point for users who want to experiment with one of the most popular and well-documented AI image generation models. The platform supports multiple Stable Diffusion variants and checkpoints, allowing users to explore different artistic styles and output qualities. Users simply enter a text prompt and receive generated images within seconds, all from their browser. Because Stable Diffusion is open-source, the online implementations benefit from a vast ecosystem of community-contributed models, LoRAs, and style presets. The service is completely free to use, though it may include usage limits or optional premium tiers for faster processing and higher resolution outputs. Stable Diffusion Online is particularly valuable for users who want to test the capabilities of open-source AI models before committing to local deployment, as well as for casual creators who need quick image generation without technical overhead.
