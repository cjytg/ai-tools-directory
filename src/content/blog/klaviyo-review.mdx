## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what Klaviyo actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

### Predictive analytics

### SMS marketing

The SMS marketing capabilities let you send targeted text messages alongside your email campaigns. The platform handles opt-in management, compliance, and delivery optimization, making it easy to reach customers on their preferred channel.
