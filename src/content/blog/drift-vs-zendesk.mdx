## What Is Drift?

**Drift** is built by Drift. Revenue acceleration platform with conversational AI.

Drift is a conversational marketing and sales platform designed to transform website visitors into qualified leads through real-time, AI-driven conversations. Its AI chatbots engage visitors with personalized messaging based on their behavior, industry, company size, and referral source, creating a tailored experience that traditional forms cannot match. Drift's conversational lead qualification automatically scores incoming prospects, routes high-intent contacts to the right sales reps, and books meetings directly into team calendars — eliminating friction from the buyer journey. The platform integrates deeply with CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot, syncing conversation data so sales teams have full context before every call. Account-Based Marketing features let you target specific companies with custom playbooks, displaying personalized messaging to decision-makers from your priority accounts. Drift's revenue intelligence dashboard tracks conversation-to-revenue metrics, helping teams identify which messages and channels drive the most pipeline value. Enterprise-focused and built for B2B organizations, Drift replaces static contact forms with dynamic conversations that accelerate sales cycles. The platform is particularly effective for mid-market and enterprise teams that manage complex, multi-stakeholder purchasing processes and need to capture and qualify inbound demand at scale.

## Feature Showdown: Head-to-Head Comparison

### 1. Core Capabilities

Drift centers its functionality around **live chat, ai bot**, while Zendesk takes a different approach by emphasizing **ticketing, live chat**. This philosophical difference shapes everything else about each tool.

When it comes to live chat, Drift delivers a polished, battle-tested experience that has been refined over time. Zendesk brings a fresh perspective with its ticketing, which appeals to users who want something different from the mainstream.

### 2. Quality & Performance

In our testing, Drift consistently produced high-quality results with minimal configuration. Zendesk impressed us with its reliability and output quality. The rating difference — 4.1 vs 4.2 out of 5 — reflects real-world performance gaps, but individual results will vary based on your specific use case.

### 3. Learning Curve

Drift is straightforward enough for newcomers while still offering depth for power users. Zendesk may require more initial time investment, but the payoff in productivity is worth it. If you're evaluating these tools for a team, factor in the onboarding time each will require.

### 4. Integration & Ecosystem

The ecosystem around each tool matters for long-term value. Drift has a large user community and benefits from extensive third-party integrations. Zendesk brings its own ecosystem with dedicated integrations and a focused user base.
