# SEMrush Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using SEMrush for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** SEMrush is comprehensive seo and digital marketing platform. At $129.95/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is SEMrush?

SEMrush is made by SEMrush, founded in 2008. It's designed for seo professionals, marketers, agencies, businesses, and it shows. The tool focuses on seo, content marketing, ppc, competitor analysis.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Keyword research**
- **Competitor analysis**
- **Site audit**
- **Content marketing**

The features are solid. I particularly like the keyword research—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $129.95/mo
- **Free tier:** No
- **Rating:** ★ 4.5/5

You'll need to pay to get started.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Most comprehensive
- Great data
- All-in-one tool

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Expensive
- Overwhelming
- Steep learning curve

To be fair, some of these cons are minor. But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use SEMrush?

If you're a seo professionals looking for seo, SEMrush is worth a look. It's particularly good for content marketing.

**Skip it if:** You're on a tight budget or need something more powerful. Check out Ahrefs, Surfer SEO instead.
