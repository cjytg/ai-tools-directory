# Gemini Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Gemini for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Gemini is a Google's AI chatbot with strong multimodal capabilities. At $20/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Gemini?

Gemini is made by Google, founded in 2023. It's designed for researchers, students, professionals, and it shows. The tool focuses on research, writing, analysis, coding.

## Key Features

- **Multimodal input**
- **Google integration**
- **Long context**
- **Real-time info**

The features are solid. I particularly like the multimodal input—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $20/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.3/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Best real-time info
- Good for research
- Free tier generous

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Less creative than GPT-4
- Inconsistent quality
- Privacy concerns

 But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Gemini?

If you're a researchers looking for research, Gemini is worth a look. It's particularly good for writing.

**Skip it if:** Check out ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft Copilot instead.
