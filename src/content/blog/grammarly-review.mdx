# Grammarly Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Grammarly for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Grammarly is ai writing assistant for grammar, clarity, and tone. At $12/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Grammarly?

Grammarly is made by Grammarly, founded in 2009. It's designed for students, professionals, writers, non-native speakers, and it shows. The tool focuses on email writing, academic writing, business communication, content editing.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Grammar check**
- **Tone detection**
- **Clarity suggestions**
- **Plagiarism check**

The features are solid. I particularly like the grammar check—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $12/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.5/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

If you're a students looking for email writing, Grammarly is worth a look. It's particularly good for academic writing.
