# Synthesia Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Synthesia for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Synthesia is an AI video generation with digital avatars for corporate content. At $22/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Synthesia?

Synthesia is made by Synthesia, founded in 2017. It's designed for enterprises, l&d teams, hr departments, and it shows. The tool focuses on training videos, corporate comms, product demos, localization.

- **Custom avatars**

The features are solid. I particularly like the ai avatars—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.
