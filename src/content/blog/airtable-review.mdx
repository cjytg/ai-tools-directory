# Airtable Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Airtable for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** Airtable is a Spreadsheet-database hybrid with rich field types. At $20/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Airtable?

Airtable is made by Airtable, founded in 2012. It's designed for teams, operations, and it works well for databases, project tracking.

## Key Features

- **Tables**
- **Views**
- **Automations**
- **Interface designer**

The features are solid. I particularly like the tables—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $20/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.5/5

## Pros

- Visual and flexible
- Good for databases
- Rich field types

## Cons

- Can be expensive
- Learning curve
- Row limits free

## Who Should Use Airtable?

If you're a teams looking for databases, Airtable is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out Coda, Notion AI instead.

## Alternatives

Coda, Notion AI are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

Airtable delivers on its promises. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.5/5**
