## What Is Perplexity?

**Perplexity** is built by Perplexity. AI-powered search engine that provides answers with sources.

Perplexity AI has carved out a unique niche as an AI-powered search engine that actually cites its sources. Unlike traditional chatbots that may hallucinate facts, Perplexity searches the live web for every query and provides numbered citations you can click to verify. This makes it invaluable for research, fact-checking, and staying current on topics. The free version works well for casual research, while Pro unlocks unlimited searches, access to multiple models including GPT-4 and Claude, and the ability to upload files for analysis. Perplexity's accuracy rate for factual queries is significantly higher than general-purpose chatbots because it grounds every response in live search results. If your primary need is finding reliable, sourced information quickly, Perplexity is worth the subscription.

## What Is You.com?

**You.com** is developed by You.com. AI-powered search engine with privacy focus and AI chat.

You.com is an AI-powered search engine that redefines how users discover information on the web. By combining real-time web access with conversational search capabilities, You.com delivers comprehensive answers complete with cited sources, allowing users to verify claims instantly. The platform goes beyond traditional text-based results by offering dedicated video and image search modes, making it a versatile tool for multimedia research. Unlike conventional search engines that simply return a list of links, You.com synthesizes information from across the web into coherent, context-aware responses tailored to your specific query. This approach saves time and reduces the need to click through multiple pages. All of these features are available completely free, with no account required for basic usage. You.com has gained popularity among students, researchers, and professionals who want AI-enhanced search without sacrificing transparency or citation integrity. Its clean, intuitive interface ensures that users of all technical skill levels can benefit from AI-assisted web discovery. Whether you are researching a complex topic, looking for visual inspiration, or simply want a smarter way to browse the web, You.com provides a modern alternative to legacy search platforms that bridges the gap between traditional search results and AI-generated answers.
