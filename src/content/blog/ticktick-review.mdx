# TickTick Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using TickTick for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** TickTick is a Task management app with built-in pomodoro timer. At $35.99/year, it's worth considering.

## What Is TickTick?

TickTick is made by TickTick, founded in 2013. It's designed for productivity enthusiasts, students, and it works well for task management, time tracking.

## Key Features

- **Tasks**
- **Pomodoro**
- **Calendar**
- **Habits**

The features are solid. I particularly like the tasks—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $35.99/year
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.3/5

## Pros

- Pomodoro built-in
- Good value
- Feature rich

## Cons

- Less polished
- Smaller community
- Limited integrations

## Who Should Use TickTick?

If you're a productivity enthusiasts looking for task management, TickTick is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out Todoist, Microsoft To Do, Notion AI instead.

## Alternatives

Todoist, Microsoft To Do, Notion AI are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

TickTick delivers on its promises. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.3/5**
