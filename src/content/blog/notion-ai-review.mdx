# Notion AI Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Notion AI for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Notion AI is ai writing assistant integrated into notion workspace. At $10/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Notion AI?

Notion AI is made by Notion, founded in 2023. It's designed for knowledge workers, students, teams, writers, and it shows. The tool focuses on note-taking, documentation, content creation, team collaboration.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Writing assistance**
- **Summarization**
- **Translation**
- **Q&A**

The features are solid. I particularly like the writing assistance—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $10/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.2/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Deep Notion integration
- Good for teams
- Multiple AI functions

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Requires Notion subscription
- Limited standalone use
- Not as powerful as ChatGPT

To be fair, some of these cons are minor. But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Notion AI?

If you're a knowledge workers looking for note-taking, Notion AI is worth a look. It's particularly good for documentation.

**Skip it if:** You're on a tight budget or need something more powerful. Check out ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper instead.
