# Ahrefs Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Ahrefs for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Ahrefs is seo toolset for link building, keyword research, and competitor analysis. At $99/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Ahrefs?

Ahrefs is made by Ahrefs, founded in 2010. It's designed for seo professionals, content marketers, agencies, and it shows. The tool focuses on seo, link building, content research, competitor analysis.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Backlink analysis**
- **Keyword research**
- **Content explorer**
- **Rank tracking**

The features are solid. I particularly like the backlink analysis—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $99/mo
- **Free tier:** No
- **Rating:** ★ 4.6/5

You'll need to pay to get started.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Best backlink data
- Great for SEO
- Comprehensive

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Expensive
- No free trial
- Complex

To be fair, some of these cons are minor. But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Ahrefs?

If you're a seo professionals looking for seo, Ahrefs is worth a look. It's particularly good for link building.

**Skip it if:** You're on a tight budget or need something more powerful. Check out SEMrush, Surfer SEO instead.
