## What Is Reclaim.ai?

**Reclaim.ai** is developed by Reclaim. AI scheduling assistant that automatically manages your calendar.

Reclaim AI is an intelligent calendar scheduling assistant that automatically organizes your meetings, tasks, and habits into an optimized weekly schedule. Instead of manually blocking time for focused work, exercise, or email catch-up, you define your priorities and constraints, and Reclaim's AI engine finds the best available slots across your calendar — dynamically adjusting when new meetings are added. The platform integrates directly with Google Calendar, pulling in your existing events and intelligently reshuffling flexible commitments to protect your focus time. Plans start at $10 per month, positioning Reclaim as a mid-range option for professionals who spend significant time juggling competing calendar demands. Key features include smart 1:1 meeting scheduling that respects both participants' preferences, habit tracking with automatic rescheduling, and team-level visibility into availability patterns. The main trade-off is that Reclaim currently works only with Google Calendar, excluding Outlook and Apple Calendar users entirely. Its AI rescheduling can also feel aggressive for users who prefer manual control over every calendar entry. Reclaim is most valuable for knowledge workers, managers, and freelancers whose calendars fragment into dozens of small blocks and who need automated defense of deep-work time.
