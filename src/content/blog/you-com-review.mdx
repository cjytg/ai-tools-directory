# You.com Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using You.com for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** You.com is ai-powered search engine with privacy focus and ai chat. At $15/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is You.com?

You.com is made by You.com, founded in 2020. It's designed for privacy-conscious users, developers, researchers, and it shows. The tool focuses on privacy search, ai research, code help, quick answers.
