## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what Otter.ai actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

### Meeting transcription

The meeting transcription joins your calls on Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams and produces accurate, real-time transcripts with speaker identification. You can also record in-person conversations through the mobile app.

### Auto notes

The automatic note-taking extracts key points, decisions, and discussion topics from your meeting transcripts. These notes are organized and shareable, eliminating the need for manual meeting minutes.

### Action items

The action item extraction identifies tasks assigned during meetings and creates a to-do list with assignees and deadlines. This ensures nothing falls through the cracks after a meeting ends.

### Integration

The Microsoft ecosystem integration connects seamlessly with Outlook, Teams, OneNote, and other Microsoft products. Tasks from flagged emails automatically appear in your to-do list, and you can add tasks directly from Teams conversations.


Beyond these core features, Otter.ai also offers a range of secondary capabilities including API access, team collaboration features, and integrations with popular platforms. The ecosystem around Otter.ai is growing, with regular updates adding new functionality based on user feedback.
