# Otter.ai Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Otter.ai for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Otter.ai is an AI meeting transcription and note-taking tool. At $16.99/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is made by Otter.ai, founded in 2016. It's designed for teams, students, journalists, professionals, and it shows. The tool focuses on meeting notes, interviews, lectures, collaboration.

## Key Features

- **Meeting transcription**
- **Auto notes**
- **Action items**
- **Integration**

The features are solid. I particularly like the meeting transcription—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $16.99/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.2/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Great for meetings
- Action items
- Easy to use

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Accuracy varies
- Limited free tier
- Privacy concerns

 But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Otter.ai?

If you're a teams looking for meeting notes, Otter.ai is worth a look. It's particularly good for interviews.

**Skip it if:** Check out Descript, Loom AI, Notion AI instead.
