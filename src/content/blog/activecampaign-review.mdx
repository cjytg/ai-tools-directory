## Features Deep Dive

### Email marketing

ActiveCampaign's email marketing capabilities include campaign creation, A/B testing, segmentation, and delivery optimization. The platform handles both broadcast campaigns and triggered transactional emails from a unified interface.

### Automation

The automation builder is genuinely sophisticated — conditional logic, split testing, goal tracking, and multi-channel triggers create customer journeys that rival enterprise marketing platforms. You can build complex workflows that respond to virtually any customer behavior.

### CRM

Integrated CRM functionality means sales teams can see marketing interactions alongside deal progress. Contacts, companies, and deals are unified with email engagement data, creating alignment that organizations using disconnected tools constantly struggle to achieve.

### Sales

Sales pipeline management features include deal tracking, task assignment, and activity logging. The CRM side integrates with marketing automation so sales reps have full context on how leads entered the pipeline and what content they've engaged with.
