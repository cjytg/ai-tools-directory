## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what Photoroom actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

### Background removal

The background removal uses AI to detect and remove image backgrounds with pixel-perfect accuracy. It handles complex edges like hair, transparent objects, and fine details that other tools struggle with.

### AI shadows

The AI shadows feature automatically generates realistic drop shadows and reflections for product images, making them look professionally photographed against any background.

### Scene generation

The scene generation creates contextual backgrounds for product photos — placing your product on a marble countertop, in a lifestyle setting, or on a colored backdrop — all automatically generated by AI.

### Batch editing

The batch editing processes hundreds of images simultaneously, applying the same edits across your entire product catalog. This is essential for e-commerce businesses that need to maintain visual consistency.
