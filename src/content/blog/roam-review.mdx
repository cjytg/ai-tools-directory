# Roam Research Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Roam Research for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** Roam Research is note-taking tool for networked thought with bi-directional links. At $15/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Roam Research?

Roam Research is made by Roam Research, founded in 2017. It's designed for researchers, academics, and it works well for research, knowledge management.

## Key Features

- **Bi-directional links**
- **Graph view**
- **Daily notes**
- **Queries**

The features are solid. I particularly like the bi-directional links—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $15/mo
- **Free tier:** No
- **Rating:** ★ 4.2/5

## Pros

- Pioneered networked notes
- Great for research
- Flexible

## Cons

- Expensive
- Learning curve
- No mobile app

## Who Should Use Roam Research?

If you're a researchers looking for research, Roam Research is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out Obsidian, Logseq, Notion AI instead.

## Alternatives

Obsidian, Logseq, Notion AI are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

Roam Research is decent but not amazing. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.2/5**
