I've used both Looka and Canva AI extensively. Here's how they actually compare—not the marketing fluff, but real usage.

## Looka: The Case For

AI-powered logo and brand identity generator.

**What I like:**
- Fast logo creation
- Affordable
- Brand kit included

**What I don't:**
- Less unique than custom
- Limited customization
- Stock-like feel

## Canva AI: The Case For

AI design tools integrated into Canva platform.

**What I like:**
- Easy to use
- Many templates
- Good for non-designers

**What I don't:**
- AI features limited
- Requires Canva subscription
- Quality varies
