## What Is Chat?

DuckDuckGo has spent over a decade building the most widely used privacy-focused search engine, offering a free alternative to Google that blocks tracking, avoids filter bubbles, and delivers clean search results without advertising manipulation. The platform's AI integration has evolved to include AI-powered answer summaries, bang shortcuts for searching specific sites directly, and instant answers that pull information from trusted sources without sending users to click through multiple results. Its commitment to privacy is foundational — DuckDuckGo doesn't build user profiles, doesn't sell search data, and doesn't personalize results based on browsing history, which means every search starts from a clean slate.

## Features Deep Dive

### AI chat

DuckDuckGo's AI chat feature lets you converse with multiple AI models — including GPT-4, Claude, and Llama — directly from the search interface. All conversations are private and not used to train models.

### Multiple models

Access to multiple AI models from different providers gives users the flexibility to compare responses and choose the model that best fits their specific query. You can switch between models within the same session.

### No tracking

DuckDuckGo's foundational commitment to privacy means AI chat conversations are not stored, linked to your identity, or used for advertising. Every session starts fresh with no personalization based on your history.

### Privacy-focused

The entire DuckDuckGo experience — search, browsing, and AI chat — operates without building user profiles or selling data. This makes it the most privacy-respecting option among AI chat services.
