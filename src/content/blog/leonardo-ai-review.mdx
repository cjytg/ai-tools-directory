# Leonardo AI Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Leonardo AI for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Leonardo AI is ai image generation platform with fine-tuning capabilities. At $12/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Leonardo AI?

Leonardo AI is made by Leonardo AI, founded in 2022. It's designed for game developers, artists, content creators, and it shows. The tool focuses on game assets, concept art, custom styles, batch generation.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-image**
- **Model training**
- **Canvas editing**
- **API access**

The features are solid. I particularly like the text-to-image—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $12/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.1/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Good free tier
- Custom model training
- Multiple styles

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Complex interface
- Inconsistent quality
- Slow on free tier

To be fair, some of these cons are minor. But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Leonardo AI?

If you're a game developers looking for game assets, Leonardo AI is worth a look. It's particularly good for concept art.

**Skip it if:** You're on a tight budget or need something more powerful. Check out Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3 instead.

## The Bottom Line

Leonardo AI is decent but not amazing. It's not perfect—the complex interface is annoying—but overall, there are better options out there.

**Final rating: 4.1/5**
