# ToDoist Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using ToDoist for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** ToDoist is a Popular task management app with natural language input. At $4/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is ToDoist?

ToDoist is made by Doist, founded in 2007. It's designed for individuals, professionals, and it works well for personal tasks, project management.

## Key Features

- **Task management**
- **Projects**
- **Labels**
- **Recurring tasks**

The features are solid. I particularly like the task management—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** $4/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.4/5

## Pros

- Simple and clean
- Cross-platform
- Good free tier

## Cons

- Limited free features
- No time tracking
- Basic collaboration

## Who Should Use ToDoist?

If you're a individuals looking for personal tasks, ToDoist is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out TickTick, Microsoft To Do, Notion AI instead.

## Alternatives

TickTick, Microsoft To Do, Notion AI are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

ToDoist delivers on its promises. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.4/5**
