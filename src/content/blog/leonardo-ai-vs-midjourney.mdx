## What Is Leonardo AI?

**Leonardo AI** is built by Leonardo AI. AI image generation platform with fine-tuning capabilities.

Leonardo AI is a comprehensive AI art generation platform designed for creators, game developers, and digital artists who need fine-grained control over their outputs. The platform offers a suite of fine-tuned models optimized for specific use cases, including game asset creation, character design, environmental art, and concept illustration. Leonardo AI provides advanced features such as image-to-image generation, canvas editing, model training on custom datasets, and real-time generation for rapid iteration. The platform's free tier grants users 150 tokens daily, allowing consistent access without payment, while the premium plan at approximately $10 per month unlocks significantly higher token allocations, priority processing, and access to all available models. Leonardo AI's model marketplace lets users discover and apply community-trained models for specialized styles and subjects. The platform strikes a balance between powerful creative tools and an approachable interface, making it suitable for both professional artists building game assets and hobbyists exploring AI-generated art.
