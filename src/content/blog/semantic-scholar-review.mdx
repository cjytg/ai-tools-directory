## What Is Scholar?

Semantic Scholar, developed by the Allen Institute for AI, has become one of the most valuable free resources for academic research by applying AI-powered analysis to over 200 million scientific papers. The platform's AI features go beyond keyword matching — it understands semantic relationships between papers, identifies influential citations, surfaces TL;DR summaries, and recommends related research based on conceptual similarity rather than just overlapping terminology.

## Features Deep Dive

### Paper search

Semantic Scholar indexes over 200 million scientific papers across all major academic disciplines. Its AI-powered search understands semantic relationships between concepts, going far beyond keyword matching to find relevant research even when terminology differs.

### Citation analysis

The platform's citation graph analysis helps researchers trace the evolution of ideas through academic literature, identify seminal papers in a field, and discover connections between research areas that might not share obvious keyword overlaps.

### Recommendations

AI-powered recommendations suggest related papers based on conceptual similarity rather than shared terminology. This helps researchers discover relevant work they might have missed through traditional keyword-based searches.

### TLDR summaries

AI-generated TL;DR summaries distill complex research papers into concise, accessible overviews. This lets researchers quickly assess whether a paper is worth reading in full before investing time in the complete text.
