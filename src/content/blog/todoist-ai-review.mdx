# Todoist AI Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Todoist AI for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Todoist AI is ai-enhanced task management with smart scheduling. At $4/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Todoist AI?

Todoist AI is made by Doist, founded in 2007. It's designed for professionals, students, teams, individuals, and it shows. The tool focuses on task management, productivity, personal organization, team tasks.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Task management**
- **AI scheduling**
- **Natural language**
- **Projects**

The features are solid. I particularly like the task management—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.
