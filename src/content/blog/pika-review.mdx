# Pika Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Pika for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Pika is ai video generation tool for creating short videos from text. At $10/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Pika?

Pika is made by Pika, founded in 2023. It's designed for content creators, marketers, social media managers, and it shows. The tool focuses on social media content, short videos, concepts, marketing clips.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-video**
- **Image-to-video**
- **Video editing**
- **Sound effects**

The features are solid. I particularly like the text-to-video—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

- **Rating:** ★ 4.2/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

If you're a content creators looking for social media content, Pika is worth a look. It's particularly good for short videos.
