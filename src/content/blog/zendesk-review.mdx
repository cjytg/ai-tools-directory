# Zendesk Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Zendesk for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Zendesk is customer service platform with ai-powered support. At $19/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Zendesk?

Zendesk is made by Zendesk, founded in 2007. It's designed for support teams, enterprises, saas companies, and it shows. The tool focuses on customer support, help desk, ticketing, self-service.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Ticketing**
- **Live chat**
- **AI answers**
- **Knowledge base**

The features are solid. I particularly like the ticketing—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $19/mo
- **Free tier:** No
- **Rating:** ★ 4.2/5

You'll need to pay to get started.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Comprehensive
- Scalable
- Good integrations

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Can be expensive
- Complex setup
- Learning curve

To be fair, some of these cons are minor. But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Zendesk?

If you're a support teams looking for customer support, Zendesk is worth a look. It's particularly good for help desk.
