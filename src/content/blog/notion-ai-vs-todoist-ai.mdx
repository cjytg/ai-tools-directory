## What Is Notion AI?

**Notion AI** is built by Notion. AI writing assistant integrated into Notion workspace.

Notion AI is an AI layer integrated directly into the Notion workspace, meaning you can summarize meeting notes, generate content, translate text, and extract action items without leaving your existing workflow. This integration is its primary advantage over standalone AI tools — the AI works on your actual Notion pages, with your actual data. Pricing is $10/month per member as an add-on to your Notion subscription. If you already use Notion as your primary workspace, the convenience of having AI built-in is significant.

## What Is Todoist AI?

**Todoist AI** is developed by Doist. AI-enhanced task management with smart scheduling.

Todoist AI enhances one of the world's most popular task management platforms with artificial intelligence that speeds up task creation, prioritization, and planning. The core product has long been valued for its clean interface, natural language input (type "every Monday at 9am" and Todoist sets the recurring schedule automatically), and cross-platform availability. The AI layer adds automatic task breakdown — turning a vague item like "plan team retreat" into actionable subtasks — smart date and priority suggestions based on your workload patterns, and AI-powered productivity insights that summarize your completed work and identify bottlenecks. Pricing starts at $4 per month for the Pro plan, which is among the lowest in the productivity software category. Todoist supports integrations with over 60 apps including Google Calendar, Slack, and Zapier, making it a flexible hub for personal and team task management. The AI features, while helpful, are still incremental rather than transformative — they streamline existing workflows rather than fundamentally changing how you organize work. The free tier is generous enough for individual users with basic needs. Todoist excels for professionals who want a reliable, fast task manager with AI features that reduce friction without overwhelming the interface.
