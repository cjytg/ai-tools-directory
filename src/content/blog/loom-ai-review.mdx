# Loom AI Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Loom AI for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Loom AI is ai-enhanced video messaging platform. At $12.50/mo, it's worth considering.

## What Is Loom AI?

Loom AI is made by Loom, founded in 2015. It's designed for remote teams, sales teams, educators, product managers, and it shows. The tool focuses on team communication, product demos, tutorials, feedback.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Video recording**
- **AI summaries**
- **Auto chapters**
- **Transcription**

The features are solid. I particularly like the video recording—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $12.50/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.3/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Easy async communication
- AI features helpful

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

If you're a remote teams looking for team communication, Loom AI is worth a look. It's particularly good for product demos.
