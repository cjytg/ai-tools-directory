## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what Loom AI actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

### Video recording

The video recording features combine screen capture with webcam overlay to create polished video messages. You can record your full screen, specific windows, or browser tabs with crystal-clear quality.

### AI summaries

The AI-generated summaries distill key takeaways from your video recordings, allowing viewers to grasp the main points without watching the full video. The summaries include timestamps for easy navigation.

### Auto chapters

The automatic chapter markers break long recordings into navigable sections. Viewers can jump to specific topics within a video, making even lengthy tutorials easy to consume.

### Transcription

The automatic transcription converts every spoken word in your recordings into searchable text. This makes your video library fully searchable and accessible to viewers who prefer reading.


Beyond these core features, Loom AI also offers a range of secondary capabilities including API access, team collaboration features, and integrations with popular platforms. The ecosystem around Loom AI is growing, with regular updates adding new functionality based on user feedback.
