## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what Kagi actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

### Ad-free search

The ad-free search experience is Kagi's core differentiator. No sponsored results, no tracking, no SEO spam cluttering your results. Every result is ranked purely on relevance, and you can customize the rankings by personally promoting or demoting entire domains.

### AI summaries

The AI-generated summaries distill key takeaways from your video recordings, allowing viewers to grasp the main points without watching the full video. The summaries include timestamps for easy navigation.

### Customizable rankings

The customizable rankings system is uniquely powerful. You can personally promote domains you trust and demote those you find unhelpful. Over time, Kagi builds a personalized search index that reflects your preferences, making every search more relevant than the last.

### Privacy-focused

Privacy is foundational to Kagi's architecture. The platform doesn't track users, sell data, or build advertising profiles. This makes it an ideal choice for anyone who values digital sovereignty and wants a search experience that serves the user, not advertisers.
