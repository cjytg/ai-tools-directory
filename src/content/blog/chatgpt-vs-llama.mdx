**What I like:**

**What I don't:**

- Needs powerful hardware
- Less polished than ChatGPT

## ChatGPT: The Case For

AI chatbot by OpenAI for conversation, writing, coding, and analysis.

**What I like:**

- Large plugin ecosystem
- Strong coding ability

**What I don't:**
