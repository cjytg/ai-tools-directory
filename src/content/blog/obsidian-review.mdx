# Obsidian Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Obsidian for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** Obsidian is knowledge management app with bidirectional linking. At Free / $50/year sync, it's worth considering.

## What Is Obsidian?

Obsidian is made by Dynalist, founded in 2020. It's designed for researchers, writers, and it works well for note-taking, knowledge base.

## Key Features

- **Markdown**
- **Bidirectional links**
- **Graph view**
- **Plugins**

The features are solid. I particularly like the markdown—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** Free / $50/year sync
- **Free tier:** Yes, completely free
- **Rating:** ★ 4.6/5

## Pros

- Free for personal
- Very flexible
- Local files

## Cons

- Learning curve
- Sync costs
- No collaboration free

## Who Should Use Obsidian?

If you're a researchers looking for note-taking, Obsidian is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out Notion AI, Roam Research, Logseq instead.

## Alternatives

Notion AI, Roam Research, Logseq are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

Obsidian delivers on its promises. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.6/5**
