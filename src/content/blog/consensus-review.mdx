## Intro

Consensus is an AI-powered research search engine specifically designed to surface and summarize evidence from scientific research papers. When you ask Consensus a question, it searches across millions of peer-reviewed studies from academic databases and returns synthesized findings with direct citations to the original research, enabling users to quickly understand what science actually says about any given topic. This evidence-based approach is particularly valuable for researchers, students, healthcare professionals, and anyone seeking to separate scientifically validated information from speculation or misinformation. Consensus highlights the degree of scientific consensus behind each finding, helping users assess the reliability and strength of the evidence. The platform supports advanced filtering by study type, publication date, and sample size, allowing for highly targeted literature searches that would otherwise require hours of manual database navigation. Its AI-generated summaries distill complex research into accessible language without sacrificing accuracy or nuance. Consensus offers a generous free tier that provides substantial access to its research database, making peer-reviewed science more accessible to the general public. By bridging the gap between academic research and everyday knowledge seekers, Consensus democratizes access to scientific evidence and promotes informed decision-making across disciplines ranging from health and medicine to psychology and environmental science.

## What Is Consensus?

Consensus has carved out a distinctive position in the search landscape by focusing exclusively on evidence-based answers derived from scientific research and academic publications. Instead of searching the general web, Consensus queries a curated database of peer-reviewed papers, extracts relevant findings, and synthesizes them into direct answers to user questions — essentially functioning as an AI research assistant for anyone who needs scientifically grounded information. The platform's ability to cite specific papers, indicate the level of scientific consensus around a finding, and distinguish between correlational and causal evidence makes it uniquely valuable for researchers, students, and informed consumers.

## Features Deep Dive

### Research paper search

Consensus searches across millions of peer-reviewed studies from academic databases, returning results organized by scientific relevance rather than keyword matching. This ensures that the most credible research surfaces first.

### AI summaries

AI-generated summaries distill complex research findings into accessible language without sacrificing accuracy. Each summary is linked to the original paper, so you can verify claims and dive deeper when needed.

### Evidence-based answers

Instead of general web results, Consensus provides direct answers synthesized from scientific literature. The platform indicates the degree of scientific consensus behind each finding, helping you assess reliability.

### Citation tracking

Every answer is accompanied by citations to the original research papers. You can click through to read the full study, check the methodology, and verify that the summary accurately represents the findings.
