## Intro

ConvertKit is a marketing tool developed by ConvertKit. Email marketing platform built for creators. Key features include email sequences, landing pages, automation, commerce. Its main strengths include creator focused, easy to use. The main drawbacks are limited templates, can be expensive. The pricing model is freemium, starting at $9/mo. It's particularly well-suited for creators, bloggers, authors. You may also want to compare it with Mailchimp and ActiveCampaign. . .

## What Is ConvertKit?

ConvertKit built its entire platform around the creator economy, and its 4.3 rating at $9 per month reflects deep alignment with the needs of bloggers, podcasters, authors, and independent content producers who need email marketing without enterprise complexity. Email sequences form the core functionality, allowing creators to automate welcome flows, course delivery, and nurturing campaigns with a visual builder that requires no technical expertise. Landing pages integrate seamlessly with email capture, and commerce features enable direct sales of digital products without leaving the platform.

## Features Deep Dive

### Email sequences

ConvertKit's email sequence builder lets you create automated email flows — welcome series, course delivery, product launches — using a visual editor that requires no coding. Each sequence can include conditional logic, delays, and subscriber segmentation.

### Landing pages

Built-in landing page creation integrates directly with email capture forms. You can build opt-in pages, sales pages, and lead magnet delivery pages without needing a separate website builder or landing page tool.

### Automation

Workflow automation features trigger actions based on subscriber behavior — link clicks, tag additions, purchases — creating personalized subscriber journeys without manual intervention.

### Commerce

ConvertKit includes commerce features for selling digital products directly. You can create product pages, handle payments, and deliver digital goods — all within the same platform used for email marketing.
