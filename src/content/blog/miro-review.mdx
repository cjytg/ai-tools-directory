## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what Miro actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

The AI brainstorming features help teams generate ideas, organize thoughts, and explore creative directions. You can prompt the AI to suggest mind map branches, generate sticky note content, or create structured frameworks for workshops.

The mind mapping tools let you visually organize ideas and concepts with branching structures. The AI can automatically expand mind maps by suggesting related concepts and connections.

### Diagramming
