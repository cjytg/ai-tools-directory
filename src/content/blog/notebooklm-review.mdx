## Features Deep Dive

Let's break down what NotebookLM actually offers and how each feature performs in practice.

### Source-grounded AI

The source-grounded AI answers questions exclusively based on the documents and sources you upload. This means zero hallucinations — every answer is traceable to your actual source material, making it ideal for research and study.

### Audio overview

The audio overview feature generates a podcast-style discussion between two AI hosts about your uploaded documents. This transforms dense material into an engaging audio experience that's easier to absorb than reading.

### Document analysis

The document analysis capability lets you ask questions about your uploaded files and get precise, source-backed answers. It handles PDFs, Google Docs, copied text, and web URLs.

### Podcast generation

The podcast generation creates conversational audio summaries of your notes and documents. Two AI voices discuss the key points, making complex information accessible and memorable.


Beyond these core features, NotebookLM also offers a range of secondary capabilities including API access, team collaboration features, and integrations with popular platforms. The ecosystem around NotebookLM is growing, with regular updates adding new functionality based on user feedback.
