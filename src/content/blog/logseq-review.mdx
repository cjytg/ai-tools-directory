# Logseq Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Logseq for a while now, and here's my honest take.

**Quick verdict:** Logseq is open-source knowledge management with bi-directional links. At Free, it's worth considering.

## What Is Logseq?

Logseq is made by Logseq, founded in 2020. It's designed for privacy advocates, researchers, and it works well for note-taking, knowledge base.

## Key Features

- **Bi-directional links**
- **Graph view**
- **Outliner**
- **Local first**

The features are solid. I particularly like the bi-directional links—it works well.

## Pricing

- **Price:** Free
- **Free tier:** Yes, completely free
- **Rating:** ★ 4.3/5

## Pros

- Free and open source
- Privacy focused
- Active community

## Cons

- Less polished
- Sync issues
- Smaller ecosystem

## Who Should Use Logseq?

If you're a privacy advocates looking for note-taking, Logseq is worth a look.

**Skip it if:** You need something different. Check out Obsidian, Roam Research, Notion AI instead.

## Alternatives

Obsidian, Roam Research, Notion AI are all options worth considering.

## The Bottom Line

Logseq delivers on its promises. I'd recommend it.

**Final rating: 4.3/5**
