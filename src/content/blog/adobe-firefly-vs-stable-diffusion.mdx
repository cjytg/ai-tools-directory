## What Is Stable Diffusion?

**Stable Diffusion** is developed by Stability AI. Open-source AI image generation model that runs locally.

Stable Diffusion Online provides web-based access to the open-source Stable Diffusion model without requiring any local installation, GPU hardware, or technical configuration. This accessibility makes it an ideal starting point for users who want to experiment with one of the most popular and well-documented AI image generation models. The platform supports multiple Stable Diffusion variants and checkpoints, allowing users to explore different artistic styles and output qualities. Users simply enter a text prompt and receive generated images within seconds, all from their browser. Because Stable Diffusion is open-source, the online implementations benefit from a vast ecosystem of community-contributed models, LoRAs, and style presets.
