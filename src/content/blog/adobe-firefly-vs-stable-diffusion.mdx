## Adobe Firefly: The Case For

**What I like:**

**What I don't:**

## Stable Diffusion: The Case For

Open-source AI image generation model that runs locally.

**What I like:**

- Highly customizable
- No content restrictions
- Local deployment

**What I don't:**

- Inconsistent quality
