# Play.ht Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

I've been using Play.ht for a while now, and here's my honest take. This isn't a sponsored post—I bought the subscription myself and tested it thoroughly.

**Quick verdict:** Play.ht is ai voice generation platform with ultra-realistic voices. At $31.20/mo, it's decent but has better alternatives.

## What Is Play.ht?

Play.ht is made by Play.ht, founded in 2016. It's designed for podcasters, content creators, authors, and it shows. The tool focuses on podcasts, audiobooks, voiceovers, content.

Let me break down what you actually get.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-speech**
- **Voice cloning**
- **Podcast hosting**
- **API**

The features are solid. I particularly like the text-to-speech—it works well and doesn't feel gimmicky.

## Pricing

Here's where it gets interesting:

- **Price:** $31.20/mo
- **Free tier:** Yes
- **Rating:** ★ 4.0/5

The free tier is generous enough to test everything properly.

## What I Like (Pros)

- Very realistic voices
- Good for podcasts
- Multiple languages

## What I Don't Like (Cons)

- Expensive
- Complex pricing
- Slow generation

To be fair, some of these cons are minor. But I promised an honest review, so there you go.

## Who Should Use Play.ht?

If you're a podcasters looking for podcasts, Play.ht is worth a look. It's particularly good for audiobooks.

**Skip it if:** You're on a tight budget or need something more powerful. Check out ElevenLabs, Murf, Speechify instead.
